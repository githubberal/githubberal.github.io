Pasta Carbonara

This creamy dish of pasta in a luxurious sauce of bacon, egg, and cheese is an easy-to-make treat. Adding lots of good, fresh black pepper will really add to the brightness of the dish and make the other ingredients "pop."

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound bacon, diced
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, minced
  • 1 pound pasta, cooked al dente
    • The traditional noodle is spaghetti, but linguini also works.
    • Don't overcook your noodles! They will become mushy.
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and fresh black pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Cook the bacon in a large pan over medium heat until crisp.
  2. Remove the bacon in a large pan over medium heat until crisp.
  3. Add the garlic and black pepper to the pan and cook for another minute.
  4. Add the bacon and pasta back to the pan and cook for another minute.
  5. Take the pan off the heat and add the eggs and cheese, stir quickly so the eggs thicken but don't scramble.
